hi, I'm a phd student in sociology at Columbia university with a background in journalism.

My doctoral research investigates social closure categories, such as race, ethnicity and nation: how individuals socially construct them, what consequences these have for inequality, and how we can design better policies to alleviate these. I use a variety of methods with a focus on computational social science, statistics, and causal inference.

I can speak catalan, spanish, english and some french with humans; R, python, sql, html, css, javascript and some Stan with computers.

I have experience teaching programming languages, statistics, and causal inference to graduate students, as well as research methods to undergrads.
